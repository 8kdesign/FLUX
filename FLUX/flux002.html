FLUX #002 - Popular

25 October 2019 (First published on 8kguy.com)

A while ago, you could use your IC to verify your membership at POPULAR Bookstores. That is no longer possible. Now, you can only use the membership card, or the QR code in POPULAR’s app. You probably have your phone with you all the time, so the app is pretty convenient. But the app’s interface isn’t.

Issue #1: POPULAR App or POPULAR Membership App?

From the layout, I get the feeling that they wanted it to be more than a POPULAR membership app. They wanted it to be a POPULAR app. The home page doesn’t show you the membership card. Instead, all you see is a huge POPULAR banner and a menu that lead to other pages, like the list of stores.

But let’s be honest: People are only getting this app as an alternative to the membership card. With the current layout, you have to tap on “Account” and scroll up a little, before you can scan the code. Doesn’t sound like much, but when you have a long queue behind you, you will understand the pain.

Issue #2: Too Cluttered for an App That Does so Little

The interface has more layers than it needs, which can bring about a bloated experience for the user. With the number of events and promotions available, the “Liked” feature is redundant. The “Notifications” page was never used. And that POPULAR banner on the home page, it is a huge waste of space.

Also, I don’t get why POPULAR has to show the coupons that have expired. Does showing the user the deals they have missed make them feel good? Not a single bit.

If you take a look at the “Promotions” and “Events” pages, you will see that there is little distinction between these 2 categories. The content on one page would also make sense on the other page. Why split them up?

Concept by 8K

Simplified Home Page

I fused the home page with the “Account” page, so the QR code is on the home page. Now you can scan it immediately after you open the app. I also removed the “Notification” and “Liked” pages, so there is no need for the navigation menu below. This new home page only has a single menu with 3 options.

Card Benefits, Events & Stores Pages

For the card benefits page, I removed the “Type of POPULAR Cards” section and brought in the coupon. It will only show the coupons that hasn’t expired. Used coupons are blurred out and stamped with “USED”. The “Birthday Privileges” section is hidden and only appears on the user’s birthday month.

Merging the old “Events” and “Promotions” page, we have the new events page.

As an alternative to the “Liked” feature, I added a “Frequently Visited” section to the stores page. Everytime you scan your membership card for payment, POPULAR knows that you have visited that store. Why not put this data to good use? This section creates a list of your most visited POPULAR stores.
